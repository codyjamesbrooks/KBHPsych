The practice of forensic psychology is complex, requiring a multidisciplinary approach and an adaptable mindset. Given the dynamic nature of the legal system, an effective forensic psychologist communicates findings to the court that reflect the present state of the legal field and are well supported by current empirical research. As a registered clinical forensic psychologist with over a decade of specialized training and applied experience, I provide expert testimony, assessment and evaluation services, and forensic consultation in the state of Colorado.

Request A Consultation

I have expertise in criminal evaluations including competency to proceed, risk assessment, criminal responsibility (e.g., sanity/mental condition), sentencing mitigation, and juvenile transfer/reverse transfer. I also have experience with civil topics, including general and neuropsychological assessment, fitness for duty evaluations, and assessment of emotional/mental injury and malingering.
